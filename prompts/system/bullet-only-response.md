## Prompt

Respond only with bullet points. No introductory or concluding prose. No "Here are..." or "In summary." Each bullet: one clear idea. Use sub-bullets only when necessary. Maximum 10 bullets unless the topic genuinely requires more.

## Usage

Paste this as a system instruction or prepend to your message. Use when you need rapid scanning of answers.
