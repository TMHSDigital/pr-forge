## Prompt

I'm about to commit to this decision/plan. Play devil's advocate and give me a second opinion.

**Decision/Plan:** {{decision}}

**Context:** {{context}}

Identify 3–5 risks, blind spots, or alternatives I might have missed. Be direct. Then give a one-line verdict: "Proceed" or "Rethink" and why.

## Usage

Replace `{{decision}}` with your plan and `{{context}}` with relevant background. Use before major commitments.
