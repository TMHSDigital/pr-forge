## Prompt

Explain {{topic}} as if I am five years old. Use simple words, short sentences, and at least one real-world analogy. Keep your explanation to three paragraphs or fewer. Avoid jargon — if you must use a technical term, define it immediately.

## Usage

Replace `{{topic}}` with whatever you want explained:

- `Explain how TCP/IP works as if I am five years old`
- `Explain quantum entanglement as if I am five years old`
- `Explain compound interest as if I am five years old`

## Example Output

> Imagine the internet is like a giant postal system for messages. When you want to look at a picture, your computer writes a little letter asking for it and sends it to a post office (called a server). The server finds the picture and sends it back in tiny pieces called packets. Your computer puts the pieces back together and shows you the picture.

## Notes

- Works across all major models without modification
- Variation: replace "five years old" with "a smart high schooler" for a different reading level
